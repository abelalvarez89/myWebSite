Citizenship Study Guide

This program is intended to help those who want to become a U.S.A citizen prepare for the exam. It is a very simple program to use that will help you learn. It uses two methods you can hear and read the questions and answers to help you learn faster.







Comparator

This program can be use to compare two lists of numbers or tow paragraph and it will tell you the words or numbers in common and in what way they differ

Quadratic Equation Solver

This simple program was made to help students check their work. This will compute an answer using quadratic equation and will decompose it to it simplest value
